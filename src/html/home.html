About

I believe when technology and design conflict the most impressive results are possible. I’ve spent my entire career as a creative technologist working with designers, strategists, coders and dreamers pushing them and having my own work pushed; our best outcomes have always been the result of conflict. The conflict of a designer imagining something completely new thus challenging technology to invent the solution, and that solution inspiring designers again and again to push their work further.

My passion and expertise is in facilitating this dance with a keen focus on technology, emerging trends and new possibilities. For over 15 years I’ve been writing code and working with others to develop digital experiences for mobile, web and physical environments. I strive to lead and inspire global teams of best in class creative technologists to create experiences that move people and make them see the world differently.

My understanding and approach to the work has always been through code, from hacked together prototypes to try an idea to battle tested production code for tens and hundreds of millions of users. I've built on REST APIs in PHP, Ruby and Node.js, physical computing experiences with Arduinos and Raspberry Pis, iOS apps, and single page applications in AngularJS among others.
