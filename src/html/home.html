About

I'm an entrepreneur, product designer, and technologist living and working in Portland Oregon. I lead creative teams with the idea that when design and technology conflict the most inspired results are inevitable. I've spent my career working with product designers, strategists, engineers and creatives pushing them and having my own work pushed. Whether at agencies, brands or startups It's always been an interdisciplinary collaboration that's lead to the best work.

In April 2012 I founded Hungry, a digital creative product agency in Brooklyn, New York to apply this approach at scale. Hungry has since grown to a team of over 20 people doing amazing work for global brands and startups alike. Before starting Hungry, I worked as a creative technologist on brands like Google, Cadillac, Jonnie Walker, Sprite, and Uniqlo at Bartle Bogle Hegarty in New York City.

My passion and expertise are in facilitating creative teams with a keen focus on technology, emerging trends, and new possibilities. For over 15 years I’ve been writing code and working with others to develop digital experiences for mobile, web and physical environments. I strive to work with the best designers, developers, and strategists to create experiences that move and inspire people and make them see the world differently.
