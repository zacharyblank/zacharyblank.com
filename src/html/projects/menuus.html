Project Menuus

In a world of many dilemmas, Menuus solves one: what to eat. The iPhone app leafs through local menus, then instantly suggests three-course meals, paired with drinks, at nearby restaurants. Open, order, eat. Simple as that.

Mennus was an internal exercise in interface design. We set out to explore mobile interactions without buttons or calls to actions and animations that would suggest to the user how to interact.
