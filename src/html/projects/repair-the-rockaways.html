Project Repair the Rockaways

Repair the Rockaways translates online activity into a real world charitable effort by donating through a digital interface. Site visitors donate money in exchange for “bricks” to help construct houses in an environment modeled after the Rockaways.

The monetary contributions are directly donated to Respond and Rebuild, a volunteer group established in the wake of Hurricane Sandy, who aided the actual Rockaways with supplies, education and labor.
